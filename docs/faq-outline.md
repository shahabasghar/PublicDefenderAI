## Proposed FAQ Sections

### 1. About the Guidance Service

**Q: What will I receive from the personalized guidance?**
- Next steps tailored to your situation
- Important deadlines and timeframes to be aware of
- Your legal rights explained in simple language
- Resources and organizations that can help
- Warnings about actions to avoid
- Legal information relevant to your case

**Q: How long does it take to generate guidance?**
- The AI analysis typically takes 30-60 seconds
- Complex cases may take up to a minute

**Q: What information do I need to provide?**
- Your jurisdiction (state/location)
- Charges or legal concerns
- Current case stage (arrest, arraignment, trial, etc.)
- Custody status
- Whether you have an attorney

---

### 2. Privacy & Security

**Q: What happens to my information?**
- Your data is never permanently stored
- Information is session-only (deleted after you close the browser)
- No account required - completely anonymous
- Automatic deletion after session ends

**Q: Is this service confidential?**
- Yes, we don't store personal identifying information
- Sessions are ephemeral and auto-deleted
- We prioritize your privacy above all else

**Q: Can I save my guidance for later?**
- Yes, use the download/print features while viewing your guidance
- Once you close the session, it's permanently deleted from our servers

---

### 3. How It Works

**Q: How does the AI guidance work?**
- We use Claude Sonnet 4 (May 2025) for complex cases
- Rule-based engine for straightforward questions
- Analyzes your situation and generates personalized advice
- Includes automatic retry logic for reliability

**Q: What's the difference between AI guidance and rule-based guidance?**
- AI guidance: For detailed, complex situations with specific circumstances
- Rule-based: For general questions and straightforward scenarios
- The system automatically chooses the best approach for your needs

**Q: How accurate is the guidance?**
- Based on verified legal information and statutes
- Uses official court data and legal databases
- However, this is NOT a substitute for a lawyer's advice

---

### 4. Getting Legal Help

**Q: How do I find a public defender?**
- Use our ZIP code search to find offices near you
- We have 123 public defender offices in our database
- View contact info, hours, and directions

**Q: What if I can't afford a lawyer?**
- Search our database of 153 legal aid organizations
- Many provide free or low-cost legal services
- Filter by your location and needs

**Q: What are diversion programs?**
- Alternative to traditional prosecution
- May avoid criminal record
- Search our database of 73 programs across major US cities

---

### 5. Case Resources

**Q: Can I search court records?**
- Yes, we provide access to RECAP Archive (free)
- Semantic search for case law
- Links to PACER for federal court documents

**Q: How do I look up criminal charges?**
- Search our database of 4,146 criminal charges
- View statute codes for all 50 states + federal
- Direct links to official state legal code pages

**Q: What legal resources are available?**
- Legal aid organizations by location
- Court locator for jurisdictions
- Rights information in simple language
- Legal glossary explaining common terms

---

### 6. Important Disclaimers

**Q: Is this service a replacement for a lawyer?**
- NO - This is educational information only
- Always consult with a licensed attorney for legal advice
- We provide guidance, not legal representation

**Q: Can I use this guidance in court?**
- This is for informational purposes only
- Court decisions require professional legal representation
- Use our resources to better understand your situation and communicate with your attorney
